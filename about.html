Trish McKenna was a nominee of the Press Photographer of The Year Award 2010 having worked as a freelance photographer providing material for newspapers and magazines across Ireland and England. Of a romantic spirit, Trish has a passion for wedding photography aiming to capture each special moment on every special day.

Yemi Abacha has 14 years experience reporting for Reuters worldwide. Deciding on a change of focus, he moved into wedding and function photography in 2010, moving to Ireland from his native UK the following year. His landscape work has been exhibited in galleries across Ireland and England to critical acclaim.
